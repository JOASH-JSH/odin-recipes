Stir-Fried Vegetables

Description

A quick and nutritious stir-fry made with a variety of colorful vegetables, cooked with garlic and ginger, and seasoned with soy sauce and optional oyster sauce. Bursting with vibrant flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Assorted vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas), sliced or chopped.
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil.
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced.
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger.
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce.
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional).
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional).

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add assorted vegetables to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using). Toss well to coat the vegetables evenly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds if desired.
  7. Serve hot as a side dish or over steamed rice for a light meal.
