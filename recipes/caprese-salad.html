Caprese Salad

Description

A light and refreshing salad featuring ripe tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves, drizzled with extra-virgin olive oil and optionally balsamic glaze. A perfect harmony of flavors and colors.

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes, sliced.
  • 1 ball fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced.
  • Fresh basil leaves.
  • Extra-virgin olive oil.
  • Balsamic glaze (optional).
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices alternately on a serving plate.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil over the salad. If desired, also drizzle with balsamic glaze for extra flavor.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.
